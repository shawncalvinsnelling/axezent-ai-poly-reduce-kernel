# Enterprise Direction

Axezent AI PRK is open source at the receipt/checker level.

Enterprise offerings can be built around:

- signed reduction certificates
- private audit vaults
- classroom/enterprise dashboards
- custom reduction packs
- integration with CI and proof-artifact pipelines
- high-throughput Rust streaming verification

Enterprise truth boundary:

Paid systems certify finite receipts under declared deterministic reduction packs. They do not certify global mathematical breakthroughs without independent formal proof and review.
